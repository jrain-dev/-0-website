We are NULLSPACE, a student-led organization based in Kennesaw State University aiming to provide a platform for students to collaboratively engage in STEM and coding-related activities. Our organization encourages skill development, knowledge sharing, and teamwork within the larger coding community on campus. Members participate in coding projects, workshops, and coding competitions. Overall, we foster a supportive environment for our members to explore, learn, and excel in various aspects of programming and software development.
